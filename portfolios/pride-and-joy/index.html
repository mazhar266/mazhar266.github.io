Who We Are

We are a Family run Christian Business based in the West Midlands. We have been trading for seven years under the ‘Care-avan’ brand name and have built up a fantastic reputation in quality Caravan and Motorhome accessories. To this end our Caravan and Motorhome cleaning products have been endorsed by some of the leading manufacturers in this field namely; Bailey Caravans & Roma Caravans and Approved by “The Swift Group”.

Having been firmly established in the Caravan market with innovative and unique, ground-breaking cleaning products we are now bring this technology into the Car market.

We have now launched our new Car Care Range called Care-avan Pride and Joy, and are proud to be endorsed by Westfield Sports Cars who are a leading sports car manufacturer in the West Midlands.

To provide to ‘Car owners’ quality Car Care products where excellence in service is apparent. We specialise in care and honesty at all customer interfaces.
